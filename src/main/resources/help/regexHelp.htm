  • Getting started
    Searchmonkey provides real-time visual results, syntax highlighting, tooltips, and undo/redo (ctrl-Z / Y) so it's easy and fun to explore Regular Expressions.

    Browse through the Reference and test different tokens to see what they do, then check out the Community to see example patterns.

    You can also Save your patterns for later reference, or to share with others.
